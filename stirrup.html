Transit Times


All of our transit information comes directly from TransportAPI.com. TransportAPI is a public-transport data service and currently manages First Bus's real-time-information system. TransportAPI has an open REST API that returns JSON formatted data, which turns into the live bus times you can see in app.

Digital Library Card


The library entrance/exit gates and the book self-check-out both feature optical readers in addition to the RFID signal readers that are used when you tap your student card. By scanning your unique card number and generating a barcode from it, you can enter the library and borrow books with only the app.

security

While Stirrup does require a yearly subscription to access some features, we never see or handle your data or account details as these are handled by your device's app store. Any information you enter to log in to the student portal or other application like Canvas is handled by your device's web browser or other app and we can't access it.
